General Questions

3ON is a professional mobile car repair and maintenance service that comes to your location. Instead of taking your car to a garage, our certified technicians bring all the necessary tools and equipment to service your vehicle wherever you are - at home, work, or anywhere else in the UAE.
We provide mobile garage services across all seven emirates of the UAE: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, and Umm Al Quwain. Our service vehicles are strategically positioned to reach you quickly.
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency roadside assistance for situations like dead batteries, flat tires, and breakdowns. For scheduled maintenance services like oil changes, we recommend booking in advance through WhatsApp or phone.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services including: Oil changes, Battery replacement and jump-start, Tire services (change, rotation, puncture repair), Brake repairs and maintenance, General mechanical repairs, Car diagnostics, Air conditioning service, and Emergency roadside assistance.
Yes, our technicians are trained to work on all major car brands including Toyota, Nissan, Honda, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Ford, Chevrolet, Hyundai, Kia, and many more. We use professional diagnostic tools compatible with most vehicle makes and models.
We offer both genuine OEM parts and high-quality aftermarket alternatives. We'll discuss your options and preferences before any work begins, ensuring you get the best value for your budget while maintaining quality and reliability.

Booking & Payment

You can book our services through: WhatsApp (fastest response), Phone call, or our website contact form. Simply describe your car issue, provide your location, and preferred time. We'll confirm your booking and send a technician to you.
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is collected after the service is completed to your satisfaction. For larger repairs, we may request a partial advance payment for parts.
For most locations within Dubai and Abu Dhabi city centers, there's no additional call-out fee. For remote areas or locations outside city limits, a small travel fee may apply. We'll always inform you of any charges upfront before confirming your booking.
